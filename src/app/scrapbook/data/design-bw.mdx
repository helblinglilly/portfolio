A test that has stuck with me since my computer science degree. The staff must have seen far too many eyesores from overenthusiastic first years 😅

<br />

I'm by no means a designer or graphic illustrator. Colour theory, contrast, composition, typography, hierarchy, all done in a responsive and accessible manner is beyond my skillset.

To keep my work accessible to people and machines, I try to keep things simple and structured. A good test is to sketch them out with pen and paper as any problems will quickly become evident.
