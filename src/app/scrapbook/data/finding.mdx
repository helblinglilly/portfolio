As humans, we can only keep a limited amount of information in our head at any given time.

Depending on what is going on in a user's life, or your own, the ability to interact with
the surrounding world might be impaired just because there is no mental memory left.

The obvious advice here is to build easy to use, relevant, intuitive systems whether they
are digital or not.

I recommend anybody apply this logic to their house. I noticed a significant improvement
in my life, being able to take on more work, after I organised my house to reduce mental clutter.
