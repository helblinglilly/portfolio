The ability to find something, be that an item in your house or a specific button on
a website is, in my opinion, and equation of :

(`urgency` + `willingness`) * `-distraction` = `emotion`.

whereby the level of distraction determines the chance of success.

High urgency and low willingness (such as having to reach out to support) will
quickly lead to frustration, whereby low urgency and high willingness creates opportunities
for moments of delight.

I consider this in the way I structure my surroundings, and in the systems I build for others.
