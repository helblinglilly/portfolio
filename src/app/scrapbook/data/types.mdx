Whenever a type is defined, it defines a contract. Defining types if you are not their authority is like expecting somebody else
to adhere to a contract that they have never seen.

I have lost many hours to Typescript lies - or rather, lies that I told myself.

Rarely, is the short term time-saving worth the trade off in the long-term.
