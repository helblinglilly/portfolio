Whenever a type is defined, it defines a contract for its consumers.

Typing data, when you are not its source, is like expecting somebody else 
to adhere to a contract that they have never seen.

I have lost many hours to type lies - or rather, lies that I told myself.

Rarely, is the short term time-saving worth the trade off in the long-term.
