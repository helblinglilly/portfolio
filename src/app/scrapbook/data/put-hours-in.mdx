Less an immediate call to action, but rather guidance for introspection.
Our actions ultimately define us, not our aspirations.

I find that this helps to set realistic, meaningful goals. Where do I spend my time, what does 
that make me and am I happy with that? Am I a good partner to my boyfriend, do I pursue my
personal interests, am I growing as a person?

That's not to say that I don't allow myself to brainrot on social media, have a lazy day,
or do otherwise generally undesireable activities. But when I do, I try to do it with 
intention rather than on autopilot.
