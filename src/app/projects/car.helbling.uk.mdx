My first proper vibe coded project, I barely ever opened a code editor for this.

I had to buy a new car, and I don't know much about them. Getting tired of notebooks, excel, and looking at a car's MOT history for a fifth time without understanding any of it got boring.

The idea was that I could input a reg and pull the MOT history, run it through an LLM for analysis and keep general notes for things like vehicle tax, the insurance price difference etc.

It felt like it worked really well. Unfortunately, I still didn't know what a broken timing chain sounded like, or a slipping clutch felt like. So while I did the best I could on paper, I ended up making a poor decision due to this confirmation bias.

I may take the site down soon as it has <s>failed to</s> serve its purpose, even though it's in read-only mode so I can try to stretch that Vercel free tier a bit further.
