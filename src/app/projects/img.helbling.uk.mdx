Little tool to strip images of their metadata. If I'm accepting user's images, or upload my own to other platforms, 
then I'd like those images to contain as little information as possible so if I, or the platform, were to get compromised,
the impact would be minimal.

Vibecoded in 10min with 1 prompt, deployed to Cloudflare workers.
